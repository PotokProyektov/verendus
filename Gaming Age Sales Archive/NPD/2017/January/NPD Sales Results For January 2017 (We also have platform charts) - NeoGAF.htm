NPD Sales Results For January 2017 (We also have platform charts)
Overall Results:

January retail game sales are down 4% year over year.

Hardware was down 19% year over year.

Console and Handheld game sales were up 14% year over year.

Physical and Steam PC software sales (for AAA games) were down 12% year over year.


Software Results:

January 2016:
  1. Resident Evil 7: Biohazard
  2. Call of Duty: Infinite Warfare
  3. Grand Theft Auto V
  4. Battlefield 1
  5. NBA 2K17
  6. Kingdom Hearts HD 2.8 Final Chapter Prologue
  7. Madden NFL 17
  8. Watch Dogs 2
  9. Overwatch*
  10. FIFA 17
*Does not include Battle.net sales

Notes:
- “Video games software spending increased by 14 percent to $313 million,” NPD analyst Sam Naji said in a statement. “This was due in large part to the stronger comparison this January with the release of Capcom’s Resident Evil 7: Biohazard.”


January Hardware:

PS4: #1
XB1: Best January Ever
NES Classic: #3 (excluding handhelds)

Notes:
- “The PlayStation 4 was the best-selling hardware (in units sold) during January 2017,” said Naji. “And for the fourth consecutive month, PlayStation 4 Slim System 500GB Uncharted 4: A Thief’s End Bundle was the top-selling PS4 sku in terms of units sold.”
